# stctm

Stellar contamination transmission spectroscopy modeling code (github.com/cpiaulet/stctm), developed by C. Piaulet-Ghorayeb et al. 2024. Designed for correcting stellar contamination in transmission spectra of M-dwarf (particularly ultracool) hosts. Incorporates the exotune sub-framework for fitting heterogeneous photosphere models to TRAPPIST-1 and similar systems. Used as the primary stellar contamination pipeline for NIRSpec PRISM observations within the NEAT GTO 1201 survey.
