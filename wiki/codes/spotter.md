# spotter

Python tool (L. J. Garcia & B. V. Rackham 2023; doi:10.5281/zenodo.10408696) for visualizing and modeling stellar-surface spot distributions — generating disk-integrated spectra given heterogeneity components, covering fractions, and (illustrative) spatial layouts, and overlaying transit chords. Useful as a companion to [[stellar-contamination-modeling]] and [[back-to-back-transit-correction]] analyses.

## Papers
- [[2025_Rathcke_TRAPPIST1bc]] — used to produce illustrative TRAPPIST-1 photospheres consistent with the inferred uniform-mixed and unocculted-cool-component scenarios, with the c transit chord highlighted.
