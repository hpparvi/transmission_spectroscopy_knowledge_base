# GENESIS

General Exoplanet Self-consistent model of Numerical Integrated Spectra (GENESIS), a 1D self-consistent radiative-convective atmospheric model developed by S. Gandhi & N. Madhusudhan (2017, 2020). Computes thermally consistent pressure-temperature profiles and emission spectra for planetary atmospheres across a wide parameter space. Used in conjunction with retrieval codes to anchor thermal structure models.
