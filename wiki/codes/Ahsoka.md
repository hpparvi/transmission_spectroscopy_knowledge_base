# Ahsoka

JWST NIRISS/SOSS time-series reduction pipeline introduced in [[2025_Gressier_WASP17b]] (Louie et al. submitted; not yet ingested). Composite design combining `jwst calibration`, [[supreme-SPOON]], `nirHiss`, and [[Eureka]] modules for stages 1–6.

## Papers

- [[2025_Gressier_WASP17b]] — introduced; one of three independent reductions for the WASP-17 b NIRISS/SOSS secondary eclipse.
