# fleck

Open-source Python package (Morris 2020; not yet ingested) for fast forward-modeling of unocculted starspots and faculae on rotating stars, producing wavelength-dependent light curves. Often paired with `emcee` for Bayesian inference of spot/facula coverage fractions and contrast temperatures.

## Papers

- [[2026_Ashtari_HATS75b]] — used in the optimized-Eureka reduction of HATS-75 b NIRSpec PRISM transits; emcee-based posteriors for spot/facula coverage and temperatures within the TLS framework.
