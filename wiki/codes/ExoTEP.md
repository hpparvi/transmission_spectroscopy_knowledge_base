# ExoTEP

JWST/HST light-curve fitting framework (Benneke et al.; not yet ingested) developed by the Montréal group, frequently paired with [[Eureka]] for transmission-spectrum extraction (the "Eureka! + ExoTEP" variant) and used as a stand-alone light-curve module in a number of NIRSpec PRISM analyses.

## Papers

- [[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]] — Eureka! + ExoTEP variant; one of four pipelines in the WASP-39 b ERS PRISM systematics study.
- [[2025_Roy_LP791-18c]] — primary light-curve fitter for the LP 791-18 c NIRSpec PRISM transit.
