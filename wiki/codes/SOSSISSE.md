# SOSSISSE

A JWST NIRISS/SOSS reduction pipeline introduced in [[2023_Lim_TRAPPIST1b]] (pipeline description in paper appendix). Developed by the Montréal group for NIRISS/SOSS time-series observations. Provides a third independent reduction pathway alongside [[supreme-SPOON]] and [[NAMELESS]], enabling cross-check consistency assessment.

## Papers
- [[2023_Lim_TRAPPIST1b]] — third cross-check reduction for TRAPPIST-1 b NIRISS/SOSS transits; first published use of SOSSISSE; pipeline described in the paper appendix.
