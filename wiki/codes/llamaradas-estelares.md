# llamaradas-estelares

Empirical stellar-flare light-curve template package (Tovar Mendoza et al. 2022; not yet ingested) providing analytic flare-shape models (rise, peak, exponential decay) for fitting in time-series photometry and spectroscopy.

## Papers

- [[2025_Howard_TRAPPIST1_flares]] — empirical flare templates fit to the six TRAPPIST-1 flares characterized in the paper, alongside the physics-based [[RADYN]] templates.
