# ScCHIMERA

Self-consistent radiative-convective + thermochemistry / photochemistry forward-modeling grid (Piskorz et al. 2018; Iyer et al. 2023; not yet ingested), an extension of the [[CHIMERA]] retrieval code that couples to the [[Photochem]] kinetics module. Used to generate physically-consistent atmospheric grids over (metallicity, C/O, Teff, recirculation, K_zz) for grid-based comparison to observed spectra.

## Papers

- [[2026_Radica_WASP96b]] — primary self-consistent grid for WASP-96 b metallicity / C/O determination and SO₂-shoreline interpretation; coupled with [[Photochem]] for kinetics.
