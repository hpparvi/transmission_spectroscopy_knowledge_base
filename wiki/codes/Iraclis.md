# Iraclis

Dedicated HST WFC3 time-series reduction pipeline for spatial-scanning observations (Tsiaras et al. 2016; not yet ingested). Handles ramp-model systematics, wavelength calibration, and spectral extraction for G141 and G102 grism data. The standard reduction tool for the ARES (Ariel Reconnaissance of Exo-atmospheres Survey) program and widely used across the HST era of exoplanet atmospheric characterization.

## Papers
- [[2021_Mugnai_GJ1132b]] — primary reduction for five WFC3 G141 transits of GJ 1132 b; flat spectrum, no molecular absorption.
