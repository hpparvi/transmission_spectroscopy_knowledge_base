# HyDRo

Hybrid Dispersion Retrieval for Rocky Planets (HyDRo), an atmospheric retrieval framework specialized for thermal emission spectra of hot rocky planets. Developed by S. Gandhi & N. Madhusudhan (2018) and extended by A. Piette et al. (2022). Handles the unique thermodynamic regime of super-Earth dayside emission, where surface-atmosphere coupling and high temperatures require dedicated radiative transfer treatment.
