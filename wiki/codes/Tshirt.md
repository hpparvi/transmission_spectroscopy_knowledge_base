# Tshirt

A JWST time-series spectroscopy reduction pipeline introduced in Roy-Perez et al. 2026 ([[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]]). Applied to the NIRSpec PRISM ERS observation of WASP-39 b as one of four independent pipelines in a systematic comparison study; pipeline choices in Tshirt contribute to the cross-pipeline abundance variation of up to 2 dex documented in that work.

## Papers

- [[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]] — introduced; one of six reductions applied to the WASP-39 b ERS PRISM dataset.
