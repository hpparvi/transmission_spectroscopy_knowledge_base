# NH₃

Ammonia — a nitrogen-bearing volatile expected in cool exoplanet atmospheres in thermochemical equilibrium for sufficiently low T (≲ 700 K). Strong absorption bands across 1–3 μm and at 10 μm. As a biosignature on temperate sub-Neptunes, NH₃ depletion (especially below the equilibrium expectation at given T, [Fe/H]) is sometimes invoked as evidence for a habitable Hycean ocean (Madhusudhan et al. 2023; not ingested). To date no firm NH₃ detection in the wiki — only weak preferences and upper limits.
