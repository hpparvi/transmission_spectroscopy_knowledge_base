# NO

Nitrogen monoxide (nitric oxide) — a high-temperature vaporization product of nitrogen-bearing rocky mineral phases. Strong mid-infrared absorption features at ~5.1 and ~4.8 μm, accessible to JWST MIRI/LRS. In the context of disintegrating rocky planets, NO is expected to form when nitrogen-bearing mineral phases (nitrides, organic-bound nitrogen) are vaporized at temperatures ≥2000 K and exposed to intense stellar radiation. Its detection would constrain the nitrogen inventory and bulk chemistry of the evaporating rocky mantle.
