# N₂O

Nitrous oxide — a nitrogen-bearing trace gas whose ~4.5 μm absorption is accessible to JWST NIRSpec G395H. A candidate biosignature (Schwieterman et al. 2022) in some contexts, though abiotic pathways exist.

## Papers
- [[2023_May_GJ1132b]] — ~100 ppm N₂O tentatively associated with a 4.5 μm transit-depth uptick in Visit 1 of GJ 1132 b; Bayesian model comparison gives only 1.4σ support and the feature is absent in Visit 2.
