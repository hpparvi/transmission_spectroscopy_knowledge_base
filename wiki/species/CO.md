# CO

Carbon monoxide; a major carbon-bearing gas in hot-Jupiter atmospheres at thermochemical equilibrium for temperatures T ≳ 1000 K. Detected in WASP-39 b's atmosphere via JWST NIRSpec PRISM (ERS 1366; original ERS team papers not yet ingested); confirmed across multiple independent reductions in [[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]].

## Papers

- [[2025_Ahrer_KELT7b]] — **not detected** on hot Jupiter KELT-7 b NIRSpec G395H (Δ ln Z = −0.7); first wiki target where CO is excluded on a hot Jupiter, plausibly due to the high cloud deck or low-metallicity scenario masking features.
- [[2025_Felix_TOI-270d]] — **not detected** on temperate sub-Neptune TOI-270 d (3σ upper limit log VMR < −4); cool atmospheric temperature (~360 K) consistent with thermochemical CO depletion in equilibrium; consistent with prior Benneke 2024 and Holmberg & Madhusudhan 2024 non-detections.
- [[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]] — confirmed detection in WASP-39 b across all four data-reduction pipelines.
- [[2026_Heinke_HATP12b]] — detected at ~8σ in HAT-P-12 b combining NIRSpec G395M + MIRI/LRS; cannot be disentangled from H₂O/CO₂ by NIRSpec alone.
- [[2026_Ashtari_HATS75b]] — detected at >3σ on HATS-75 b NIRSpec PRISM (TLS framework).
- [[2001_Brown_TransmissionDiagnostics]] — predicts the CO first-overtone band at 2.3 μm as the "most promising" molecular diagnostic on HD 209458 b (modest H₂O contamination).
- [[2025_Crouzet_HATP12b]] — detected at 4.1σ on HAT-P-12 b NIRSpec G395M.
- [[2025_AcunaAguirre_WASP80b]] — present on WASP-80 b in joint interior–atmosphere retrieval.
- [[2025_Barat_V1298Tau-b]] — detected at 10σ on the young sub-Neptune progenitor V1298 Tau b; CO/CH₄ ratio used as constraint on quench-point depth in the atmosphere; consistent with the 500 K T_int + K_zz ~ 10⁷–10⁸ inference.
