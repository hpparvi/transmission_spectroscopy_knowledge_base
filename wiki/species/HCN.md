# HCN

Hydrogen cyanide. Predicted as a tracer of carbon-to-oxygen-ratio elevation and as a prebiotic precursor on early-Earth-analog atmospheres. Possesses a sharp near-infrared band around 3 μm accessible to [[JWST-NIRSpec]] G395H. Has not been firmly detected on any JWST target in the wiki; appears most often as a *ruled-out* species in retrievals of high-S/N hot-Jupiter and warm-Neptune spectra. Historically, an HCN signal was claimed pre-JWST on GJ 1132 b by Swain et al. 2021 (not ingested) but was retracted via independent HST/WFC3 reanalysis in [[2021_Mugnai_GJ1132b]] and then ruled out at JWST precision in [[2025_Bennett_GJ1132b]].

## Detection status
- **No firm detection** in any wiki paper.
- **Ruled out / upper-limited:**
  - [[GJ1132b]] — pre-JWST claim (Swain 2021) retracted by both HST reanalysis ([[2021_Mugnai_GJ1132b]]) and four-visit JWST coadd ([[2025_Bennett_GJ1132b]]).
  - [[K2-18b]] — log VMR < −2.54 (95%) in 13-species comprehensive retrieval ([[2025_FernandezRodriguez_K2-18b]]); not preferred in ensemble retrieval ([[2025_Schmidt_K2-18b]]).
  - [[HD209458b]] — 3σ upper limit log VMR < −6.97 ([[2025_Verma_HD209458b]]); rejects Giacobbe et al. 2021 ground-based claim.
  - [[WASP-94Ab]] — upper-limited in NIRSpec G395H free-chemistry retrieval ([[2025_Ahrer_WASP94Ab]]).
- Considered as a potential biosignature/prebiotic indicator in the [[2025_Seager_biosignature-prospects]] perspective; flagged as having significant abiotic-source false-positive concerns.
