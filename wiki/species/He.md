# He

Atomic helium. The metastable **He I 2³S–2³P triplet at 1083.0 nm** is populated via stellar EUV photoionization and recombination cascade; the metastable lower level traps population, making the line an unusually deep absorption feature in extended hot-Jupiter exospheres. Predicted as a transmission tracer in [[2000_Seager_TheoryTransmission]]; first observed nearly two decades later in escaping-atmosphere studies (Spake et al. 2018, Nortmann et al. 2018; both not yet ingested).

## Papers

- [[2000_Seager_TheoryTransmission]] — foundational prediction of the metastable He I 1083 nm line as a transmission feature for close-in giant planets; potentially extreme amplitude if the exosphere is extended.
