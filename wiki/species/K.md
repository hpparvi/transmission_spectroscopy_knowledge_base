# K

Atomic potassium; an alkali metal with a prominent doublet at ~770 nm; a companion detection to [[Na]] in hot-Jupiter transmission spectra. Detected in WASP-39 b by the original ERS team (not yet ingested) and confirmed across multiple independent reductions in [[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]].

## Papers

- [[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]] — confirmed detection in WASP-39 b across independent reductions of the NIRSpec PRISM ERS dataset.
- [[2001_Brown_TransmissionDiagnostics]] — Brown 2001 predictions for K resonance doublet at 767 nm on HD 209458 b; line cores ~10⁻³ relative to stellar continuum.
- [[2000_Seager_TheoryTransmission]] — foundational prediction of the K I resonance doublet at 767.0 nm as a companion transmission feature to Na I.
