# FeH

Iron monohydride; a refractory hydride species detected in M-dwarf and brown-dwarf atmospheres and inferred in some hot-Jupiter dayside spectra. Tentatively detected in WASP-17 b's dayside NIRISS/SOSS emission spectrum at ~3σ in [[2025_Gressier_WASP17b]] (positive eclipse-depth priors); only an upper limit when negative depths are allowed.
