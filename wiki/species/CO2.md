# CO₂

Carbon dioxide — a metallicity-sensitive tracer with a strong 4.3 μm absorption feature accessible to JWST NIRSpec G395H.

## Papers
- [[2025_Bennett_GJ1132b]] — coadded four-visit GJ 1132 b spectrum rejects pure-CO₂ atmospheres at > 3σ; joint with MIRI/LRS emission (Xue et al. 2024; not yet ingested), CO₂ is constrained to < 10 mbar.
- [[2025_Alam_L168-9b]] — pure-CO₂ atmosphere forward models tested on L 168-9 b; a MIRI eclipse at 15 μm is proposed as the path to confirming a CO₂-dominated atmosphere if one exists.
- [[2024_WeinerMansfield_GJ486b]] — MIRI/LRS emission of Gl 486 b rules out Venus-like thick CO₂ atmospheres; only <1 ppm CO₂ at ≤10 bar allowed.
- [[2024_Wachiraphan_LTT1445Ab]] — MIRI/LRS emission of LTT 1445 A b rules out pure 1/10/100 bar CO₂ atmospheres at 4.2σ/6.6σ/6.8σ respectively.
- [[2024_Scarsdale_L98-59c]] — COMPASS transmission of L 98-59 c rules out H₂/CO₂ mixtures with ≥10% CO₂ (MMW ≤8 g/mol) at 3σ; high-MMW CO₂-rich atmospheres remain compatible.
- [[2023_May_GJ1132b]] — pure-CO₂ atmosphere forward models tested as a scenario for GJ 1132 b Visit 1; provide a poorer fit than H₂O-dominated atmospheres, and G395H alone cannot constrain CO₂ coexistence with H₂O.
- [[2023_Moran_GJ486b]] — pure-CO₂ atmospheres on GJ 486 b ruled out to 6.5σ ([[Eureka]]) / 2.3σ (other reductions); upper limits on CO₂ mixing ratio in retrievals are stringent but permit CO₂ superseding water in forward models.
- [[2023_Lustig-Yaeger_LHS475b]] — pure-CO₂ atmospheres remain consistent with the featureless LHS 475 b spectrum (χ² ≈ 1.0); 3σ upper limit of 49 ppm on any absorption feature at 4.3 μm.
