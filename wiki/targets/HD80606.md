# HD 80606

A bright (V ≈ 9, J = 7.7) Sun-like G5V dwarf (T_eff = 5565 ± 92 K, R = 1.066 R☉, M = 1.047 M☉, [Fe/H] = +0.348) hosting the highly eccentric hot Jupiter [[HD80606b]]. Member of a wide-binary system with HD 80607; the binary architecture has been invoked as a Lidov-Kozai-induced source of HD 80606 b's extreme eccentricity. Quiet, mature host; stellar contamination is not a primary systematic for atmospheric characterization at this T_eff.
