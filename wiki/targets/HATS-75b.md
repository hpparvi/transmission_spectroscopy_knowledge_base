# HATS-75 b

Saturn-mass gas giant (Mp = 0.491 ± 0.039 MJ, Rp = 0.88 ± 0.01 RJ, P = 2.79 d) orbiting the early-M dwarf [[HATS-75]] (Jordán et al. 2022; not yet ingested). The first wiki target in the **[[GEMS]]** class — Giant Exoplanets around M-dwarf Stars. Three NIRSpec PRISM transits ([[JWST-GO-3171]]) yield a transmission spectrum jointly shaped by atmospheric absorption and stellar contamination ([[2026_Ashtari_HATS75b]]); within a TLS framework, retrievals find sub-solar atmospheric metallicity (log [M/H] = −1.74) with super-solar C/O ≈ 1.04, in tension with a much higher bulk metallicity Z_p ≈ 0.20.

## Atmospheric composition

- **Detected (TLS framework):** [[CH4]], [[CO]], [[CO2]] (>3σ; [[2026_Ashtari_HATS75b]]).
- **Upper limit:** [[H2O]] — likely masked by stellar contamination.
- **Atmospheric metallicity:** log [M/H] = −1.74 +0.92/−0.76 — sub-solar (~1% solar).
- **C/O:** 1.04 +0.40/−0.09 — super-solar, near unity.
- **Bulk planetary metallicity:** Z_p ≈ 0.20 — implies inefficient vertical mixing.
