# GJ 1132

A nearby (~12.6 pc) M4.5 dwarf hosting the rocky super-Earth [[GJ1132b]]. Commonly cited stellar parameters: Teff ≈ 3270 K, R ≈ 0.21 R☉, log g ≈ 5.0, [Fe/H] ≈ −0.12.
