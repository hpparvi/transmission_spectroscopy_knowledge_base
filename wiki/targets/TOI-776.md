# TOI-776

A bright (J=8.48) M1V dwarf — catalogued as LP 961-53 — hosting a multi-planet system: the super-Earth [[TOI-776b]] (inner, 8.25-day period) and the water-world candidate [[TOI-776c]] (outer, 15.67-day period). Discovered by Luque et al. 2021 (not yet ingested); updated masses/radii from Fridlund et al. 2024 (not yet ingested).
