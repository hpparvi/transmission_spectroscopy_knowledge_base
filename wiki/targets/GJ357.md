# GJ 357

A nearby (~9.44 pc) M2.5V dwarf (Teff ≈ 3505 K, M = 0.342 M☉, R = 0.360 R☉, [Fe/H] = −0.02; Luque et al. 2019, not yet ingested) hosting at least three planets, of which only [[GJ357b]] (the innermost) transits. Exceptionally inactive for an M2.5V star: log R'HK = 5.53 (one of the least active stars in a sample of >4000 M-dwarfs; Boro Saikia et al. 2018, not yet ingested), rotation period ~78 ± 2 days. XUV luminosity estimated sub-threshold for rapid atmospheric escape since ~10³ Myr ([[2025_Taylor_GJ357b]]). System age ≳4 Gyr by MORS rotation-track estimate.
