# TRAPPIST-1 c

The second of the seven TRAPPIST-1 transiting terrestrial planets (Gillon et al. 2016, 2017; not yet ingested), on a 2.42-day orbit around the M8V ultracool dwarf [[TRAPPIST-1]].

## Observations to date

- **JWST NIRSpec/PRISM** ([[2025_Rathcke_TRAPPIST1bc]]): the c transit was observed quasi-simultaneously with [[TRAPPIST-1b]] and corrected for [[stellar-contamination]] using the b spectrum via [[back-to-back-transit-correction]], achieving a 2.5× red-noise reduction at 0.8–2 μm.
- **JWST MIRI F1500W** secondary eclipses (4 visits, GO 2304; original Zieba et al. 2023 not yet ingested) reanalyzed with FN-PCA in [[2025_Connors_MIRI-15um]]: joint depth 312 ± 128 ppm, T_day = 344 +43/−52 K. The FN-PCA dayside is ~36 K **cooler** than Zieba et al. 2023's 380 ± 31 K — a ~1σ shift in the atmosphere-favoring direction (potential tension; see [[2025_Connors_MIRI-15um]]). Status compatible with either a semireflective bare rock or a thin O₂/CO₂ atmosphere.
