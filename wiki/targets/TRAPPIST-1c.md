# TRAPPIST-1 c

The second of the seven TRAPPIST-1 transiting terrestrial planets (Gillon et al. 2016, 2017; not yet ingested), on a 2.42-day orbit around the M8V ultracool dwarf [[TRAPPIST-1]]. Prior JWST/MIRI emission work (Zieba et al. 2023; not yet ingested) disfavors thick atmospheres. In [[2025_Rathcke_TRAPPIST1bc]], the c transit was observed quasi-simultaneously with [[TRAPPIST-1b]] and corrected for [[stellar-contamination]] using the b spectrum via [[back-to-back-transit-correction]], achieving a 2.5× red-noise reduction at 0.8–2 μm.
