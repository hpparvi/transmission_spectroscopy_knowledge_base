# TOI-836 b

A super-Earth (Rp=1.70 R⊕, Mp=4.5 M⊕, Teq=871 K) on a 3.82-day orbit around the bright K-dwarf [[TOI-836]] (HIP 73427). Inner planet of the TOI-836 multi-planet system; the outer companion TOI-836 c (1 R⊕ larger) is a sub-Neptune with a very different atmospheric character. Target of the JWST COMPASS program.
