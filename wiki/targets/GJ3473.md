# GJ 3473

Mid-M dwarf (T_eff = 3347 ± 54 K, R⋆ = 0.364 ± 0.012 R☉, distance 27.3 pc) hosting the rocky planet [[GJ3473b]]. Reanalyzed for atmospheric retention via four MIRI F1500W eclipses in the Hot Rocks Survey ([[JWST-GO-3730]]).
