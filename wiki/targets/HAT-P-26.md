# HAT-P-26

K-dwarf (T_eff = 5079 K, R = 0.87 R☉, [Fe/H] = +0.05; Hartman et al. 2011, Valenti et al. 2025 in prep) hosting the warm Neptune-mass planet [[HAT-P-26b]]. Quiet host with no strong activity signatures; the [[2025_Gressier_HATP26b]] [[POSEIDON]]_TLS retrieval finds no statistical preference for stellar contamination (Bayes factor < 1).
