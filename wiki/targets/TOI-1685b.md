# TOI-1685 b

A hot ultra-short-period super-Earth orbiting an M2.5V dwarf with Teq ≈ 1062 K; bulk density consistent with a rocky, iron-rich composition. Five JWST NIRSpec G395H transits yield a flat transmission spectrum indicating bare rock or very high mean molecular weight atmosphere.
