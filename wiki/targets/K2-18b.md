# K2-18 b

Habitable-zone sub-Neptune (Rp ≈ 2.61 R⊕, Mp ≈ 8.63 M⊕, T_eq ≈ 255 K) on a 32.9-day orbit around the M3 dwarf [[K2-18]] (Foreman-Mackey et al. 2015, Montet et al. 2015; both not yet ingested). Famously a **Hycean-candidate** with disputed claimed JWST detections of CH₄, CO₂, and DMS (Madhusudhan et al. 2023, 2025; not yet ingested). The wiki currently has K2-18 b only as a comparative reference in [[2025_Roy_LP791-18c]], which contrasts K2-18 b's clear CH₄+CO₂ spectrum with LP 791-18 c's hazy methane-only spectrum despite similar bulk parameters.

## Atmospheric composition (per Roy 2025 references; primary detection papers not yet ingested)

- [[CH4]] and [[CO2]] both present and at log CH₄/CO₂ = −0.3 ± 0.8 — inverted relative to the temperature expectation when compared to TOI-270 d.
- **DMS / dimethyl sulfide** controversy: Madhusudhan et al. 2023, 2025 (not yet ingested) claim a tentative biosignature detection; multiple subsequent reanalyses dispute it. Not adjudicated within the current wiki.
- **CO₂ detection** itself remains contested in the literature; per [[2025_Roy_LP791-18c]] it is supported by GO 2372 reanalysis (Hu et al., in prep; not yet ingested).
