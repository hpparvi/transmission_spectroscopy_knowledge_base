# GJ 3929 b

Near-Earth-sized rocky planet (1.09 ± 0.04 R⊕, 1.13 ± 0.09 M⊕) on a 2.616-day orbit around the nearby M3.5V dwarf [[GJ3929]] (Teq ≈ 568 K). JWST MIRI F1500W photometry at 15 μm finds T_day = 782 ± 79 K and temperature ratio R ≈ 1.07 ± 0.10, consistent with a bare rock; CO₂-dominated atmospheres > 100 mbar excluded at >3σ ([[2025_Xue_GJ3929b]]).
