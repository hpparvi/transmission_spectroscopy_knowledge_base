# TOI-270

M3V dwarf (T_eff = 3506 ± 70 K) hosting the multi-planet [[TOI-270]] system, including the warm super-Earth [[TOI-270b]] and the temperate sub-Neptune [[TOI-270d]]. NIRPS-GTO high-resolution NIR spectroscopy gives [M/H] = −0.13 ± 0.07 and Fe/Mg = 0.81 ± 0.22 (comparable to solar) ([[2025_Coulombe_TOI-270b]]). Low magnetic activity (rotation period ~58 d, photometric modulation ~1 ppt in TESS) is consistent with the lack of detectable transit-light-source contamination across two simultaneous-planet visits, making TOI-270 an unusually clean system for atmospheric characterization of small planets.
