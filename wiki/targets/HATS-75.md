# HATS-75

Early M dwarf (T_eff ≈ 3790 K, M⋆ ≈ 0.6 M☉, log g = 4.68) hosting the Saturn-mass [[HATS-75b]]. Stellar activity (rotation, spot-crossings during transit) provides independent evidence for unocculted spots+faculae that are degenerate with atmospheric features in the planet's NIRSpec PRISM spectrum.
