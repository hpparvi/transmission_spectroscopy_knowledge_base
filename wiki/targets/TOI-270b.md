# TOI-270 b

A warm super-Earth (Rₚ = 1.306 ± 0.028 R⊕, Mₚ = 1.48 ± 0.18 M⊕, ρₚ = 3.7 ± 0.5 g cm⁻³, T_eq,A=0 = 569 K, P = 3.36 d) — the innermost member of the M3V system [[TOI-270]]. The first JWST visit yielded a planetary-radius update that places its bulk density **4.4σ below an Earth-like composition**, requiring a percent-level water mass fraction. The transmission spectrum shows a tentative H₂O absorption feature near 3 μm, with stellar contamination ruled out via the simultaneous transit of [[TOI-270d]] in the same visit — a methodological first ([[2025_Coulombe_TOI-270b]]).
