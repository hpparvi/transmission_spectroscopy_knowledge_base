# GJ 3090

M2V dwarf at 22.4 pc; T_eff = 3556 ± 70 K, M = 0.519 ± 0.013 M☉, R = 0.516 ± 0.016 R☉, [Fe/H] = −0.06 ± 0.12, P_rot ≈ 17.65 d, age ≈ 1 Gyr (Almenara et al. 2022; not yet ingested). Moderately active per HARPS activity indicators. Hosts the warm sub-Neptune [[GJ3090b]] and an outer 13-day RV-only candidate (∼17 M⊕ minimum mass; non-transiting).
