# GJ 3470 b

Warm Neptune (M = 0.044 M_J ≈ 14 M_⊕, T_eq ≈ 615 K) around M1.5 dwarf [[GJ3470]]. JWST NIRCam F322W2+F444W transmission spectrum reported by Beatty et al. 2024 (not ingested) — the lowest-T_eq, lowest-mass anchor in the [[2025_Fu_statistical-trends]] 8-planet population sample. **SO₂-L = 0.32 ± 0.12** (one of two positive-SO₂-L outliers, alongside [[WASP-107b]]); CO₂-L = 0.94 ± 0.12. Bulk Z fraction 0.84 ± 0.05 (one of the most metal-rich planets in the sample). Joins the cool low-mass corner of the SO₂-shoreline parameter space; comparable T_eq + mass to [[2025_Crossfield_SO2shoreline]] grid endpoints.
