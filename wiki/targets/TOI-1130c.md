# TOI-1130 c

Outer hot Jupiter of the [[TOI-1130]] system (13 ± 0.4 R⊕ ≈ 1.16 R_J, 336 ± 5 M⊕ ≈ 1.06 M_J, P = 8.35 d). In **2:1 mean-motion resonance** with the inner mini-Neptune [[TOI-1130b]]. The orbital architecture (hot Jupiter with inner mini-Neptune companion in 2:1 MMR) is essentially unique among the ~7 % of hot-Jupiter systems with inner companions (Sha 2026 not ingested), and strongly favors disc-driven migration over high-eccentricity migration. No JWST atmospheric characterization to date; the TOI-1130 c planet is most often referenced in this wiki as the **outer pebble-filtering hot Jupiter** that constrains the formation interpretation of TOI-1130 b ([[2026_Barat_TOI1130b]]).
