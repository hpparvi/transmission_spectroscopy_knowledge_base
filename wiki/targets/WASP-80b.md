# WASP-80 b

Warm gas giant (Mp = 0.554 +0.030/−0.039 M_J, Rp = 0.952 ± 0.027 R_J, Teq ≈ 825 K) on a 3.07-day orbit around the late-K / early-M dwarf [[WASP-80]]. Targeted by HST + JWST panchromatic transmission (0.5–4 μm) and emission (1–12 μm) observations (sourced from Bell et al. 2023, Wiser et al. 2025, Jacobs et al. 2023; all not yet ingested) which have confirmed [[H2O]], [[CH4]], [[CO]], [[CO2]] in the atmosphere. The wiki's primary entry on WASP-80 b is the [[2025_AcunaAguirre_WASP80b]] **joint interior + atmosphere retrieval**, which establishes two fiducial scenarios bracketing the bulk and atmospheric metallicity (Z_p = 0.12–0.28; M/H = 2.75–10× solar).

## Atmospheric composition

- **Detected** (per cited input retrievals): [[H2O]], [[CH4]], [[CO]], [[CO2]] (Wiser et al. 2025; not yet ingested).
- **Atmospheric metallicity:** 2.75 +0.88/−0.56 × solar (secular-cooling JR6) or 10.00 +8.20/−4.75 × solar (additionally-heated JR5*) ([[2025_AcunaAguirre_WASP80b]]).
- **C/O:** 0.12 (JR6) or 0.24 (JR5*) — sub-solar in both scenarios.
- **Bulk planetary metallicity Z_p:** 0.12 ± 0.02 (JR6) or 0.28 ± 0.11 (JR5*) — brackets the Thorngren et al. 2016 mass-metallicity trend.

## Limb asymmetry

[[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]] finds both limbs muted (δA_H = 0.64 ± 0.49 H — formally consistent with zero) on the GO 5924 NIRISS/SOSS transit, placing WASP-80 b in the cool-and-symmetric corner of the proposed [[limb-asymmetry]] horizon at T_eq = 825 K.
