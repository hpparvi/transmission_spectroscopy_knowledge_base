# LHS 475

A nearby, low-activity M3.5V dwarf (Teff=3300 K, R=0.2789 R☉) hosting the Earth-sized rocky planet [[LHS475b]]. High proper motion (1.28 arcsec/yr); no archival source confusion identified back to 1999 DSS imagery.
