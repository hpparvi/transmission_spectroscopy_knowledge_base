# TOI-836 c

Sub-Neptune sibling to [[TOI-836b]] in the [[TOI-836]] multi-planet system. JWST NIRSpec G395H transmission spectrum included in the uniform COMPASS reanalysis ([[2025_Gordon_COMPASS-G395H]]); spectrum is **flat** with metallicity lower limit of several hundred × solar, and TOI-836 c is interpreted as a hazy-sub-Neptune analog to GJ 1214 b. Statistically significant NRS1/NRS2 transit-depth offset documented.
