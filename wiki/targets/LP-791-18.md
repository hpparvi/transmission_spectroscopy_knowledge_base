# LP 791-18

M6 dwarf (R⋆ = 0.182 R☉, distance = 26.65 pc) hosting at least three planets including the temperate sub-Neptune [[LP-791-18c]]. The brightness and small size make LP 791-18 c's NIRSpec PRISM transmission spectrum competitive with the K2-18 / TOI-270 sub-Neptune benchmarks despite the cooler host.
