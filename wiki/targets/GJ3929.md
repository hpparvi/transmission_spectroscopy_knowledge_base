# GJ 3929

Nearby M3.5V dwarf hosting at least one confirmed transiting rocky planet ([[GJ3929b]]) and two additional planet candidates (c at 14.994 days, d at 6.116 days; from MAROON-X RVs in [[2025_Xue_GJ3929b]]). Stellar parameters from Kempton et al. 2021 (not yet ingested) and Suárez Mascareño et al. 2022 (not yet ingested).
