# WASP-94 A

F8 dwarf (T_eff ≈ 6170 K), the primary of the WASP-94 visual binary, hosting the inflated hot Jupiter [[WASP-94Ab]]. The B component (WASP-94 B) hosts a separate hot Jupiter (WASP-94 Bb).
