# WASP-94 A

F8 dwarf (T_eff ≈ 6194 K, M ≈ 1.45 M☉, R ≈ 1.48 R☉, age ≈ 2.55 Gyr; Teske 2016, not yet ingested), primary of the WASP-94 visual binary, hosting the inflated retrograde hot Jupiter [[WASP-94Ab]]. The B component (WASP-94 B) hosts a separate non-transiting hot Jupiter (WASP-94 Bb). WASP-94 A is **above the Kraft break** — its hot Jupiter's retrograde orbit is therefore primordial, making it diagnostic of high-eccentricity migration. Stellar abundances (Teske 2016 + [[2025_Ahrer_WASP94Ab]]): metallicity 2.09 × solar; C/O★ = 0.68 ± 0.10 — the reference values against which atmospheric abundances of [[WASP-94Ab]] are normalized.
