# TRAPPIST-1 g

Outer habitable-zone rocky planet of the [[TRAPPIST-1]] system (Gillon et al. 2017; not yet ingested). JWST NIRISS/SOSS observations under [[JWST-GO-2589]] (Lim) provide the source data analyzed for **flare contamination** in [[2025_Howard_TRAPPIST1_flares]]; no atmospheric detection or rejection claim has yet appeared in the wiki for this planet.
