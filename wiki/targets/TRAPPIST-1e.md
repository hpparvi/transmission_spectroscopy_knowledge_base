# TRAPPIST-1 e

Third potentially habitable-zone rocky planet in the [[TRAPPIST-1]] system (Gillon et al. 2017; not yet ingested). With Teq ≈ 251 K and a mass/radius consistent with a rocky interior (0.692 M⊕, 0.920 R⊕, Agol et al. 2021; not yet ingested), it is among the most compelling targets for secondary-atmosphere searches.
