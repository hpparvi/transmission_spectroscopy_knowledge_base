# TOI-561

An ancient (~10 Gyr) metal-poor ([Fe/H] ≈ −0.4) K dwarf in the Galactic thick disk, hosting the ultra-short-period super-Earth [[TOI-561b]]. Among the oldest known planet-hosting stars with JWST observations.
