# LHS 475 b

An Earth-sized rocky exoplanet (Rp=0.99 R⊕; mass unmeasured, estimated ~0.91 M⊕ from interior modeling) on a 2.03-day orbit around the nearby M3.5V dwarf [[LHS475]] (Teq=586 K). Originally detected as TESS candidate TOI 910.01 and validated via JWST transit observations. Target of the JWST CHAMPs program.
