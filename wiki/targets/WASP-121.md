# WASP-121

F-type host (T_eff ≈ 6459 K; M ≈ 1.35 M☉, R ≈ 1.46 R☉; Bourrier et al. 2020, not yet ingested) of the benchmark ultra-hot Jupiter [[WASP-121b]]. Sits well above the Kraft break — observed obliquity is therefore primordial.
