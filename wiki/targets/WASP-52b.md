# WASP-52 b

Inflated hot Jupiter (Mp ≈ 0.46 MJ, Rp ≈ 1.27 RJ → log g = 2.85 cgs, T_eq ≈ 1301 K, P = 1.75 d) around the active K dwarf [[WASP-52]]. Of the nine planets in the [[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]] sample, the only one with a *negative* δA_H = −0.62 ± 0.41 H — clearer morning, muted evening — opposite the trend in the three high-asymmetry planets, but at less than 2σ; merits follow-up.
