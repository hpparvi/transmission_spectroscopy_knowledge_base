# TOI-270 d

Temperate sub-Neptune (T_eq ≈ 384 K) in the [[TOI-270]] multi-planet system. Comparative reference target in [[2025_Roy_LP791-18c]]; clear spectrum with both [[CH4]] and [[CO2]] detected (Mikal-Evans et al. 2023; Holmberg & Madhusudhan 2024; both not yet ingested). Log CH₄/CO₂ = 0.95 +0.69/−0.52 — inverted vs the temperature expectation when compared to [[K2-18b]], suggesting different bulk H₂O/H₂ ratios (formation history) rather than only temperature controlling sub-Neptune composition.
