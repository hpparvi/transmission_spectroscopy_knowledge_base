# GJ 436

Bright nearby M3V dwarf (T_eff = 3648 K, R_⋆ = 0.449 R_⊙, log g = 4.47, [M/H] = +0.5 from JWST stellar fit; Bourrier et al. 2018 + Knutson 2014b + JWST fit; not ingested). At 9.75 pc; J = 6.07. Hosts the archetype warm-Neptune [[GJ436b]] on a 2.64-day eccentric orbit. The stellar parameters were updated by [[2025_Mukherjee_GJ436b]] using the JWST-derived stellar SED + BOSZ stellar atmosphere models (Mészáros et al. 2024; not ingested).

The high stellar metallicity ([M/H] = +0.5) is unusual for the M-dwarf population and is consistent with the inferred high atmospheric metallicity of GJ 436 b.
