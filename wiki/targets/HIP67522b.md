# HIP 67522 b

Young, low-density (M = 0.047 M_J ≈ 15 M_⊕, R = 0.93 R_J), warm sub-Saturn around the **24-Myr T-Tauri-stage K-dwarf [[HIP67522]]**. JWST/NIRSpec G395H transmission spectrum reported by Thao et al. (submitted; not ingested), included in [[2025_Fu_statistical-trends]] as the only young-planet entry in the 8-planet population sample. SO₂-L = −0.71 ± 0.15; CO₂-L = 3.80 ± 0.18 (the second-highest CO₂-L in the sample after WASP-107 b). Bulk Z fraction 0.7 ± 0.06 — comparable to WASP-107 b at much lower T_eq.
