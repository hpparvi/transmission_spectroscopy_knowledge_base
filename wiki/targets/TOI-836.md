# TOI-836

A bright (K=6.8) K-dwarf (Teff=4552 K, R=0.665 R☉, [Fe/H]=-0.28) — catalogued as HIP 73427 — hosting a small multi-planet system. The inner planet [[TOI-836b]] is a super-Earth; the outer planet TOI-836 c is a sub-Neptune (Wallack et al. 2024; not yet ingested). Discovered in Hawthorn et al. 2023 (not yet ingested).
