# HD 209458

G0V star (T_eff ≈ 6065 K, R⋆ ≈ 1.20 R☉, M⋆ ≈ 1.10 M☉, near-solar metallicity) hosting the first transiting exoplanet [[HD-209458b]]. Stellar parameters from Mazeh et al. 2000 / Brown 2001 (not yet ingested); the brightness of the host (V ≈ 7.65) made the first-ever transit detection feasible.
