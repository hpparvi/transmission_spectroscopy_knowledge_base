# WASP-17

F6 dwarf (Teff ≈ 6550 K, R⋆ = 1.49 R☉, [M/H] = −0.25, log g = 4.2) hosting the inflated retrograde hot Jupiter [[WASP-17b]]. Stellar rotation period ~8 d. The first F-dwarf host in this wiki — extends the host-star spectral coverage beyond M, K, and G dwarfs.
