# TOI-1468 b

Rocky planet around the M dwarf [[TOI-1468]]; three MIRI F1500W eclipses ([[JWST-GO-3730]] Hot Rocks Survey; original Valdés et al. 2025 not yet ingested) reanalyzed in [[2025_Connors_MIRI-15um]] yield a joint eclipse depth of 286 ± 39 ppm and T_day = 964 +78/−83 K — **hotter than any plausible bare-rock prediction**. The deep-eclipse origin is unexplained; remains an open anomaly.
