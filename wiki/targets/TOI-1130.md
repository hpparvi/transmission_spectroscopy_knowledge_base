# TOI-1130

K-dwarf (T_eff ≈ 4360 K, M ≈ 0.71 M☉, R ≈ 0.697 R☉, [Fe/H] = +0.11; Huang et al. 2020a not ingested; Korth et al. 2023 not ingested) hosting the **unique 2:1 mean-motion-resonance system** of an inner mini-Neptune ([[TOI-1130b]]; 3.66 R⊕, 4.07-d period) and an outer hot Jupiter ([[TOI-1130c]]; 13 R⊕, 8.35-d period). Only ~7 % of hot-Jupiter systems have inner companions (Sha et al. 2026 not ingested), making TOI-1130 a rare and well-constrained formation-history laboratory. Visit-to-visit TTV amplitude reaches ~2 hours, requiring careful transit timing for JWST observations.
