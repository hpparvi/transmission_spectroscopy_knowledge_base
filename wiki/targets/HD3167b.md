# HD 3167 b

Ultra-short-period super-Earth (P ≈ 0.96 d) in the [[HD3167]] multi-planet system; a lava world with Teq ≈ 1786 K and bulk density consistent with a rocky interior (ρ ≈ 5.5 ± 0.5 g cm⁻³). JWST MIRI/LRS secondary eclipse indicates a dayside emission far below the bare-rock prediction, providing ~5σ evidence for either a high-albedo surface or a heat-redistributing atmosphere ([[2026_Coy_HD3167b]]).
