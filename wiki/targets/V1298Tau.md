# V1298 Tau

Weak-lined T Tauri star (K0–K2-ish, T_eff ≈ 5050 K, R⋆ ≈ 1.34 R☉, M⋆ ≈ 1.18 M☉) in the Taurus-Auriga moving group, age ~10–30 Myr. Hosts four planets (b, c, d, plus an outer candidate; David et al. 2019a; not ingested), with the inner three in near 2:3:6 mean-motion resonance. Rotation period ~2.8 days; magnetic field strength 100–300 Gauss (Finociety et al. 2023; not ingested). Active host with 4–5% peak-to-peak photometric variability in the R band. RV jitter from stellar activity prevents direct planet mass measurements ([[2025_Barat_V1298Tau-b]] uses atmospheric scale height as an alternative).
