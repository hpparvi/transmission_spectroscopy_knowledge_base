# WASP-94 Ab

Inflated hot Jupiter (Mp ≈ 0.50 MJ, Rp ≈ 1.58 RJ → log g = 2.7 cgs, T_eq ≈ 1543 K, P = 3.95 d) around the F8 primary of the WASP-94 visual binary [[WASP-94A]]. **The strongest morning–evening limb-asymmetry signal** in the [[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]] sample: δA_H = 2.83 ± 0.34 H, with a featureless morning limb and prominent water absorption on the evening limb — consistent with high-altitude aerosols (~10⁻⁵ bar) cleared from the evening side by downwelling flow or dayside cloud evaporation. Used as the LSM normalization reference planet.
