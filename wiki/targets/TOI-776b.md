# TOI-776 b

A warm super-Earth (Rp=1.85 R⊕, Mp=4.0 M⊕, Teq~520 K) on an 8.25-day orbit around the bright M-dwarf [[TOI-776]] (LP 961-53; J=8.48). Inner planet of the TOI-776 multi-planet system; the outer planet [[TOI-776c]] is also a JWST COMPASS target ([[2025_Teske_TOI776c]]). Discovered by Luque et al. 2021 (not yet ingested).
