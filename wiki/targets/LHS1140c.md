# LHS 1140 c

Inner rocky planet of the [[LHS1140]] system; three MIRI F1500W eclipses ([[JWST-GO-3730]] Hot Rocks Survey; original Fortune et al. 2025 not yet ingested) reanalyzed in [[2025_Connors_MIRI-15um]] yield a joint eclipse depth of 242 ± 35 ppm and T_day = 528 +32/−36 K, ruling out a thick atmosphere and consistent with a low-albedo bare rock.
