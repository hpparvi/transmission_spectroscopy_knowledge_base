# HAT-P-18 b

Warm Saturn (Mp ≈ 0.20 MJ, Rp ≈ 1.00 RJ → log g = 2.69 cgs, T_eq ≈ 848 K) around K dwarf [[HAT-P-18]]. Limb-asymmetry analysis ([[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]]) finds both limbs muted (δA_H = 0.27 ± 0.45 H), consistent with the cool-end of the proposed [[limb-asymmetry]] horizon.
