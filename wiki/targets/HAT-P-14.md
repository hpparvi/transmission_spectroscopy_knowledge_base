# HAT-P-14

F-type star (T_eff = 6600 K, log g = 4.25, [Fe/H] = 0.11; Bonomo et al. 2017, Stassun et al. 2017; not ingested) hosting the super-Jupiter [[HAT-P-14b]]. Bright (J = 9.09). **Inactive** — no detectable Ca H/K emission; minimal stellar contamination expected (per Rackham et al. 2019 (= [[2019_Rackham_TLS-FGK]]) FGK spot-coverage population statistics). Has a known faint M-dwarf companion at 0.844" (ΔJ ≈ 5 mag) which is captured in the NIRISS SOSS subarray; its dilution effect on the planet's transmission spectrum is ≤0.8% across 0.6–2.8 μm and does not produce a water signal mimic ([[2025_Liu_HATP14b]] Appendix).
