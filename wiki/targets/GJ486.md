# GJ 486 (= Gl 486)

A nearby (~8 pc) bright M3.5 dwarf (Teff=3317 K, R=0.324 R☉, M=0.312 M☉, [Fe/H]=-0.15) hosting the hot rocky planet [[GJ486b]]. Shows high XUV activity over the system's ≥6.6 Gyr age (Diamond-Lowe et al. 2024; not yet ingested) despite appearing relatively inactive at optical wavelengths.
