# GJ 1214

Nearby (14.6 pc) M4.5 dwarf hosting the archetype thick-aerosol sub-Neptune [[GJ1214b]] (Charbonneau et al. 2009; not ingested). T_eff = 3250 K, log g = 5.026, [Fe/H] = 0.29 (Cloutier et al. 2021; not ingested). Modestly metal-rich among the M-dwarfs in the wiki. Stellar UV SED for photochemistry retrievals on GJ 1214 b is taken from the MUSCLES survey (France et al. 2016; not ingested).
