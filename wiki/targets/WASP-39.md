# WASP-39

Solar-type G dwarf (Teff ≈ 5400 K) hosting the hot Jupiter [[WASP-39b]], the primary target of the JWST Early Release Science transmission-spectroscopy program ([[JWST-ERS-1366]]). Mildly irradiated stellar flux makes WASP-39 b a favorable candidate for detailed atmospheric characterization.
