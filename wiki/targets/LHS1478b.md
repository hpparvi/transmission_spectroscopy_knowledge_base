# LHS 1478 b

Rocky planet around the M-dwarf [[LHS1478]]; observed in two MIRI F1500W eclipse visits as part of the [[JWST-GO-3730]] Hot Rocks Survey (PI: August). Visit 2 was a nondetection; the joint-fit FN-PCA reanalysis ([[2025_Connors_MIRI-15um]]) yields a marginal eclipse depth of 86 ± 66 ppm, ruling out a dark bare rock at ~2σ. Consistent with either a CO₂-bearing atmosphere or a high-albedo (A_B ≈ 0.66) bare rock; awaits GO-7675 follow-up for resolution.
