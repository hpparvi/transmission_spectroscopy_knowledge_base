# WASP-127 b

Warm sub-Saturn (M = 0.165 M_J, R = 1.31 R_J, P = 4.18 d, T_eq = 1420 K) around the G-dwarf [[WASP-127]] (Lam et al. 2017; not ingested). Low-density (ρ ≈ 0.08 g cm⁻³) — among the puffiest warm sub-Saturns known. **First JWST/NIRSpec G395H transmission spectrum** reported in [[2025_Fu_statistical-trends]] (GO 2437, PI Pelletier; 2023 May 8). Observation suffered ~10% slit-loss flux deficit but JWST pointing stability (~0.01 pixel) preserved spectroscopic precision. In the population sample: SO₂-L = −1.55 ± 0.28 (the lowest SO₂-L of the 8 planets); CO₂-L = 2.64 ± 0.39. Anomalously low SO₂ inferred to reflect either insufficient sulfur photochemistry at T_eq ≈ 1420 K or atmospheric scenario beyond the simple PICASO+Photochem grid.
