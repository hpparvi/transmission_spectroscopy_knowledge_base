# L 98-59 c

The middle of three transiting rocky planets around the M3V dwarf [[L98-59]]. Radius 1.385 R⊕, mass 2.22 M⊕, Teq ≈ 553 K on a 3.69-day orbit. Part of the JWST COMPASS program sample and of the broader effort to probe atmospheric retention in multi-planet M-dwarf systems.
