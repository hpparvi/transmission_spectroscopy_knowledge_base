# TRAPPIST-1 f

Habitable-zone rocky planet of the [[TRAPPIST-1]] system (Gillon et al. 2017; not yet ingested). JWST NIRSpec PRISM transit visits under [[JWST-GTO-1201]] (Lafrenière, NEAT) provide the source data analyzed for **flare contamination** in [[2025_Howard_TRAPPIST1_flares]]; no atmospheric detection or rejection claim has yet appeared in the wiki for this planet.
