# LHS 1140

Nearby M dwarf hosting at least two transiting planets including the rocky [[LHS1140c]]. Outer LHS 1140 b (a habitable-zone candidate) is observed in the Hot Rocks Survey but not analyzed in the wiki's current ingest set.
