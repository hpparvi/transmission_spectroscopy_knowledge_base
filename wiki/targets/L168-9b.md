# L 168-9 b

A highly-irradiated rocky super-Earth (Rp=1.39 R⊕, Mp=4.60 M⊕, Teq=981 K) on a 1.4-day orbit around the bright M1V dwarf [[L168-9]] (K=7.1). Discovered by Astudillo-Defru et al. 2020 (not yet ingested) as TOI-134 b; a key JWST COMPASS target for atmospheric characterization.
