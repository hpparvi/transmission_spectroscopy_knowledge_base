# LTT 1445 A

The primary star of the nearby (6.86 pc) triple M-dwarf system LTT 1445. M-type, Teff=3340 K, R=0.271 R☉, M=0.257 M☉, [Fe/H]=-0.34. Hosts two known transiting rocky planets; companions LTT 1445 B and C form a close pair ~7 arcsec away. Parameters from Pass et al. 2023 and Winters et al. 2022 (not yet ingested).
