# Spitzer-MIPS

The Multiband Imaging Photometer for Spitzer; 24 μm channel used for hot-Jupiter eclipse + phase-curve photometry. Single-band, low-cadence; the 24 μm anchor extends panchromatic transmission/eclipse spectra to the mid-IR. Superseded by [[JWST-MIRI]] LRS / F1500W.

## Papers

- [[2013_Pont_HD189733b]] — 24 μm transit depth on [[HD-189733b]] (Knutson et al. 2009; not ingested); 1.55 ± 0.94 × 10⁻⁴ uncertainty represents the long-wavelength anchor of the panchromatic spectrum.
