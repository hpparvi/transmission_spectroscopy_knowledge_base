# NIRPS

Near-InfraRed Planet Searcher (Bouchy et al. 2025; Artigau et al. 2024) — fiber-fed near-infrared (0.98–1.80 μm) high-resolution (R ≈ 70 000) spectrograph on the ESO 3.6 m at La Silla, operating in parallel with HARPS. In the wiki it appears as a **host-star characterization** complement to JWST: spectroscopy of M-dwarf hosts under the NIRPS-GTO sub-programs yields stellar abundances (Fe/Mg, [M/H]) that feed interior-structure priors used to convert JWST radii into water mass fractions and core mass fractions.

## Papers
- [[2025_Coulombe_TOI-270b]] — six visits of TOI-270 c and d (NIRPS-GTO Prog 112.25P3.001; PIs Bouchy & Doyon) coadded into a single high-SNR stellar spectrum yielding [M/H] = −0.13 ± 0.07 and Fe/Mg = 0.81 ± 0.22, used as the Gaussian CMFI prior for the [[smint]] interior fit of [[TOI-270b]].
