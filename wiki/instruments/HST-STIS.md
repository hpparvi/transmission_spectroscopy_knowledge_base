# HST-STIS

The Space Telescope Imaging Spectrograph on Hubble (Woodgate et al. 1998; not yet ingested). The G430L (0.29–0.57 μm) and G750L (0.52–1.02 μm) spectroscopic modes are the workhorse for HST optical transmission spectroscopy of exoplanets — providing Na, K, and Rayleigh-slope coverage that complements JWST near- and mid-infrared modes.
