# VLT-FORS2

The FOcal Reducer and low-dispersion Spectrograph 2 on ESO's Very Large Telescope (Appenzeller et al. 1998; not yet ingested). Workhorse for ground-based optical (0.36–1.0 μm) transmission spectroscopy of exoplanets in the pre-JWST era; provides Na, K, and Rayleigh-slope constraints not accessible to JWST/NIRSpec, complementing JWST near-/mid-infrared transit observations.

## Papers

- [[2026_Radica_WASP96b]] — archival VLT/FORS2 spectrum (Nikolov et al. 2018; not yet ingested) at 0.36–0.82 μm combined with JWST NIRSpec/G395H + NIRISS/SOSS for panchromatic retrieval; the FORS2 data drive the Na detection.
