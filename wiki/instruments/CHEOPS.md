# CHEOPS

The ESA Characterising Exoplanet Satellite, a 0.32 m photometric space telescope in sun-synchronous low-Earth orbit, covering 330–1100 nm (broadband optical). Designed for high-precision photometry of known exoplanet systems; not a spectrograph. Used in conjunction with JWST for simultaneous optical coverage during MIRI transit observations.
