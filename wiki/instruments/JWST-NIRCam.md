# JWST-NIRCam

The Near-Infrared Camera on JWST (Rieke et al. 2023; not yet ingested), with grism time-series modes (F322W2 covering ~2.4–4.0 μm and F444W covering ~4.0–5.0 μm) for transit and eclipse spectroscopy of bright targets. Less commonly used than [[JWST-NIRSpec]] for small-planet transmission, but valuable for hot/warm-Jupiter transmission and emission, particularly in combination with NIRSpec and MIRI for panchromatic coverage.

## Papers

- [[2025_AcunaAguirre_WASP80b]] — archival JWST NIRCam transmission (2.5–4.0 μm) and emission (F322W2 + F444W) on WASP-80 b combined with HST and JWST/MIRI in a joint interior–atmosphere retrieval.
