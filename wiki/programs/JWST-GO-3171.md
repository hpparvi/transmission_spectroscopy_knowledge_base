# JWST-GO-3171

JWST Cycle 2 GO program 3171 (PIs: S. Kanodia, C. I. Cañas, J. Libby-Roberts), titled "Red Dwarfs and the Seven Giants: First Insights Into the Atmospheres of Giant Exoplanets around M-dwarf Stars". A NIRSpec PRISM transmission-spectroscopy survey of seven [[GEMS]] (Giant Exoplanets around M-dwarf Stars) targets, addressing the joint challenges of M-dwarf [[stellar-contamination]] and giant-planet chemistry. Survey website: gemsjwst.github.io.

## Papers

- [[2026_Ashtari_HATS75b]] — first published GEMS-JWST result; three PRISM transits of HATS-75 b; sub-solar atmospheric metallicity in a TLS framework.
