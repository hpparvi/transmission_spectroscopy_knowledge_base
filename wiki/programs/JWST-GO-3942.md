# JWST GO 3942

JWST Cycle 2 GO program 3942 (PI: M. Damiano). Four-transit NIRSpec/G395H transmission-spectroscopy campaign of the sub-Earth [[L98-59b]] designed to search for a tidally-heated volcanically-outgassed atmosphere. The observations probe SO₂ and other volcanic-outgassing-product features at 2.87–5.15 μm.

## Papers

- [[2025_BelloArufe_L98-59b]] — primary result; SO₂-rich atmosphere preferred over flat line at 3.3–3.8σ across three retrieval codes (ExoTR, Aurora, POSEIDON); first [[volcanic-atmosphere]] inference in the wiki.
