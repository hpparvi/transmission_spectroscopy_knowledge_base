# JWST COM 2734

JWST commissioning program 2734; NIRISS/SOSS transmission observations of hot Jupiters [[HAT-P-18b]] and [[WASP-96b]] (G. Fu et al. 2022; M. Fournier-Tondreau et al. 2023; M. Radica et al. 2023; J. Taylor et al. 2023). The WASP-96 b dataset originally established the NIRISS/SOSS mode for JWST exoplanet transmission spectroscopy.

## Papers
- [[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]] — uses archival COM 2734 NIRISS/SOSS data on HAT-P-18 b and WASP-96 b as part of the nine-planet uniform reanalysis.
- [[2026_Radica_WASP96b]] — uses the COM 2734 WASP-96 b NIRISS dataset alongside new NIRSpec G395H + VLT/FORS2 transits.
