# JWST PID 1541

JWST commissioning program 1541 (PI: Espinoza). NIRISS/SOSS observation of the super-Jupiter [[HAT-P-14b]] as a NIRISS time-series-spectroscopy calibration target on 2022-06-08. GR700XD/CLEAR + SUBSTRIP256, 6 groups × 572 integrations. Originally analyzed by Albert et al. 2023 (A23, not ingested) and Espinoza et al. 2023 (E23, not ingested), which reported a blueward slope + 1.4 μm "break" that could not be explained. [[2025_Liu_HATP14b]] reanalyzes with the latest STScI jwst pipeline + [[FIREFLy]] — **the blueward slope disappears**, attributed to improvements in the jwst pipeline (notably superbias subtraction) since A23. The result is a corroborating data point for [[2025_Carter_NIRISS-jump-ramp]] arguing for uniform reanalysis of early NIRISS/SOSS commissioning datasets.
