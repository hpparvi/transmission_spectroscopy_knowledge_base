# JWST GO 1743

JWST Cycle 1 Guest Observer program 1743 (PI: M. Mansfield), obtaining two MIRI/LRS secondary-eclipse observations of the rocky planet [[GJ486b]] to test for atmospheric retention via [[secondary-eclipse-spectroscopy]]. Delivered a precise dayside temperature and, combined with earlier transmission work, a shoreline-relevant constraint.

## Papers
- [[2024_WeinerMansfield_GJ486b]] — two MIRI/LRS eclipses of Gl 486 b; concludes no thick atmosphere.
