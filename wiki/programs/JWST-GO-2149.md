# JWST-GO-2149

JWST Cycle 1 GO program 2149 (PI: J.-M. Désert), targeting the young (10–30 Myr) sub-Neptune progenitor V1298 Tau b with a single primary transit using NIRSpec Bright Object Time-Series Spectroscopy in G395H/SUB2048 mode (2.85–5.15 μm). Single visit on 2023 February 12; 14.7 hr science observation with 10,250 integrations of 4.51 s each. Used in conjunction with archival HST/WFC3 G141 (Barat et al. 2024b; not ingested) for panchromatic 1.0–5.2 μm coverage.

## Papers

- [[2025_Barat_V1298Tau-b]] — primary scientific output; first JWST atmospheric characterization of a 10–30 Myr planet; CO₂ at 35σ + H₂O at 30σ + CO + CH₄ + SO₂; ~0.6× solar metallicity; subsolar C/O ≈ 0.22; high T_int ≈ 500 K from CH₄ thermometer; mass measurement (12 ± 1 M⊕) via atmospheric scale height retiring the 200 M⊕ RV upper limit at 40σ.
