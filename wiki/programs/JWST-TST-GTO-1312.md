# JWST TST GTO 1312 — DREAMS Warm Neptune

JWST Cycle 1 GTO program 1312 (PI: N. Lewis), part of the JWST Telescope Scientist Team (JWST-TST) DREAMS — Deep Reconnaissance of Exoplanet Atmospheres through Multi-instrument Spectroscopy — series. Targets the Warm Neptune class via [[HAT-P-26b]]; complementary to GTO 1331 (DREAMS terrestrial — TRAPPIST-1) and GTO 1353 (DREAMS hot Jupiter — WASP-17 b). The single NIRSpec G395H transit yielded the first SO₂ detection in a Neptune-mass planet ([[2025_Gressier_HATP26b]]); forthcoming NIRISS/SOSS and panchromatic MIRI/LRS analyses are expected to refine the metallicity and C/O constraints.

## Papers

- [[2025_Gressier_HATP26b]] — single NIRSpec G395H transit of HAT-P-26 b on 2023 June 15; SO₂ at lnB = 13.46, CO₂ at lnB = 85.64, H₂O at lnB = 4.06; ~10× solar metallicity, sub-solar C/O = 0.14.
