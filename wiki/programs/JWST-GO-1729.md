# JWST GO 1729

JWST Cycle 1 GO program 1729 (PI: T. M. Evans-Soma; co-PI: T. Kataria); NIRSpec G395H **full phase-curve** observation of the benchmark ultra-hot Jupiter [[WASP-121b]] (37.8 hr continuous observation, 2.54–5.15 μm). The dataset has been analyzed in multiple ways — phase-curve emission (Evans-Soma 2025, not ingested), prior NIRSpec/G395H transmission via spot-checks (Mikal-Evans 2023, not ingested), and the **transit-only-from-phase-curve** extraction in [[2025_Gapp_WASP121b]], which detects [[SiO]] at 5.2σ and dayside H₂O thermal dissociation.

## Papers

- [[2025_Gapp_WASP121b]] — transit-only extraction; first definitive identification of the SiO NUV absorber via direct G395H spectral signature; introduces a custom dayside/nightside split [[NEMESISPY]] retrieval.
