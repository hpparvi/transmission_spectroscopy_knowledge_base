# JWST GO 2708

JWST Cycle 1 Guest Observer program 2708 (PI: Z. Berta-Thompson), obtaining three MIRI/LRS secondary-eclipse observations of the nearby rocky planet [[LTT1445Ab]] to probe thermal emission and atmospheric retention via [[secondary-eclipse-spectroscopy]].

## Papers
- [[2024_Wachiraphan_LTT1445Ab]] — three MIRI/LRS eclipses of LTT 1445 A b; rules out thick atmospheres (100 bar CO₂ at >6σ).
