# JWST GO 3385

JWST Cycle 2 GO 3385 (PI: Saugata Barat) — atmospheric characterization of the inner mini-Neptune of the **TOI-1130 2:1 mean-motion-resonance system**. Comprises one [[JWST-NIRSpec]] G395H transit on 2024 Aug 20 and one [[JWST-NIRISS]] SOSS transit on 2024 Aug 24, both of [[TOI-1130b]]. The simultaneous CHEOPS GTO observation (CH_PR149003, PI: Brandeker; under [[CHEOPS]]) overlaps the NIRISS visit to provide optical baseline.

The science goal is to test the correlation between planet formation location and atmospheric composition by characterizing a planet in a system with a well-constrained formation history (disc migration is essentially required by the 2:1 MMR + inner mini-Neptune + outer hot Jupiter architecture). Per [[2026_Barat_TOI1130b]], the first results detect H₂O, CO₂, SO₂ (tentative CH₄) and yield μ ≈ 5.5 amu and [M/H] ≈ 60× solar — consistent with ex-situ formation beyond the water ice line + disc migration.
