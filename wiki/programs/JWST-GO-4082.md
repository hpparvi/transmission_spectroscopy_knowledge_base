# JWST-GO-4082

JWST Cycle 2 GO program 4082 (PI: M. Radica), a single-transit NIRSpec G395H observation of [[WASP-96b]]. Combined with archival JWST NIRISS/SOSS (ERS) and VLT/FORS2 data for panchromatic retrieval analysis.

## Papers

- [[2026_Radica_WASP96b]] — H₂O, CO₂, Na detections; tentative SO₂ at lnB = 2.69; super-stellar metallicity.
