# JWST-GO-3315

JWST Cycle 2 GO program 3315 (PI: J. T. Wright, Science PI: N. Tusay), targeting the disintegrating rocky planet K2-22 b with MIRI/LRS mid-infrared transit spectroscopy. Four transit windows observed 2024 April 24–27 to characterize the dust and gas composition of the escaping material.

## Papers
- [[2025_Tusay_K2-22b]] — four MIRI/LRS transit windows of K2-22 b; one clear 9.7σ detection; iron-rich silicate dust; tentative NO gas absorption at ~5.1 μm.
