# JWST DD 9235 — Rocky Worlds DDT

JWST Director's Discretionary Time program 9235 (PIs: N. Espinoza & H. Diamond-Lowe), targeting a sample of small rocky planets with MIRI photometric imaging for secondary-eclipse characterization. First publication targets GJ 3929 b with F1500W (15 μm) eclipse photometry.

## Papers
- [[2025_Xue_GJ3929b]] — two MIRI F1500W (photometric imaging) secondary eclipses of GJ 3929 b; T_day = 782 ± 79 K, R = 1.07 ± 0.10; bare-rock consistent; CO₂ > 100 mbar ruled out.
