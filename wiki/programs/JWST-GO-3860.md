# JWST-GO-3860

JWST GO program 3860 (PI: J. Teske), targeting TOI-561 b with NIRSpec G395H secondary eclipse spectroscopy. Four secondary eclipses were obtained on May 1–3, 2024, yielding the first detection consistent with a thick atmosphere on a dense rocky exoplanet.

## Papers
- [[2025_Teske_TOI561b]] — four NIRSpec G395H secondary eclipses of TOI-561 b; dayside T_day = 1800–2150 K; thick atmosphere interpretation.
