# JWST-GO-4818

JWST Cycle 3 GO program 4818 (PI: Weiner Mansfield), the LAVA LAMPS survey (Lava-world Atmospheric Variability and Abun-dances with MIRI LRS; Planetary Science survey). A systematic MIRI/LRS secondary-eclipse survey targeting ultra-short-period rocky planets (lava worlds) with the goal of characterizing the presence or absence of volatile-rich atmospheres via the T_day/T_max ratio (R diagnostic).

## Papers

- [[2026_Coy_HD3167b]] — first published result; single MIRI/LRS eclipse of HD 3167 b; R = 0.57; ~5σ atmospheric evidence.
