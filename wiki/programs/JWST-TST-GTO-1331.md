# JWST GTO 1331 — DREAMS

JWST Telescope Scientist Team (TST) Guaranteed Time Observations program 1331 (PI: M. Mountain), "Deep Reconnaissance of Exoplanet Atmospheres using Multi-instrument Spectroscopy." Targets multiple TRAPPIST-1 planets with NIRSpec PRISM for population-level atmospheric characterization. First publication targets the habitable-zone planet TRAPPIST-1 e.

## Papers
- [[2025_Glidden_TRAPPIST1e]] — four JWST NIRSpec PRISM transits of TRAPPIST-1 e; featureless spectrum; H₂-rich and thick CO₂ atmospheres ruled out; secondary atmosphere cannot be confirmed or excluded.
- [[2025_Espinoza_TRAPPIST1e]] — data reduction and GP stellar contamination companion to Glidden; novel GP marginalization over photospheric heterogeneity; cloudy H₂-dominated atmospheres ruled out >3σ.
