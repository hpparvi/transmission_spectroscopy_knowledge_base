# JWST-GTO-1281

JWST Cycle 1 GTO program 1281 (PIs: P.-O. Lagage, J. Bouwman), the **ExoMIRI** subgroup of the MIRI European Consortium. Multi-instrument transmission spectroscopy of the warm sub-Saturn [[HAT-P-12b]] across NIRISS/SOSS, NIRSpec/G395M BOTS, and MIRI/LRS — designed to anchor information-content-style retrieval analyses spanning 0.6–12.4 μm.

## Papers

- [[2025_Crouzet_HATP12b]] — foundational NIRSpec G395M paper for HAT-P-12 b; CO₂ at 12.2σ, H₂O at 6.0σ, CO at 4.1σ; metallicity ~10× solar with photochemistry, ~60× without.
- [[2026_Heinke_HATP12b]] — panchromatic information-content extension; combines this program's NIRISS/SOSS, NIRSpec G395M (Crouzet 2025), and MIRI/LRS data with HST archival; H₂O, CO₂, CO, H₂S detections.
