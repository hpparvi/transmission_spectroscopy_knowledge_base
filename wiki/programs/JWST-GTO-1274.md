# JWST GTO 1274

JWST Cycle 1 Guaranteed Time Observations program 1274 (PI: J. Lunine), targeting the rocky super-Earth [[GJ1132b]] with MIRI/LRS secondary-eclipse spectroscopy to characterize its dayside thermal emission and constrain atmospheric retention.

## Papers
- [[2024_Xue_GJ1132b]] — single MIRI/LRS secondary eclipse of GJ 1132 b; T_day = 709 ± 31 K, R = 0.95 ± 0.04; bare-rock interpretation; rules out Earth-like and Venus-like atmospheres.
