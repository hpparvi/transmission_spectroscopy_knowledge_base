# JWST PID 1118

JWST commissioning program 1118 (PI: Proffitt). NIRSpec G395H BOTS observation of the super-Jupiter [[HAT-P-14b]] as a NIRSpec time-series-spectroscopy calibration target on 2022-05-30. The data have been analyzed twice in the wiki — first by Espinoza et al. 2023 (E23, not ingested) reporting a featureless spectrum, then re-reduced by [[2025_Liu_HATP14b]] with the latest STScI jwst pipeline + [[FIREFLy]], revealing small water bumps at 1.4, 1.9, and 2.6 μm. The reanalysis is paired with the [[JWST-PID-1541]] NIRISS/SOSS commissioning observation of the same target.

## Papers

- [[2025_Liu_HATP14b]] — combined PID 1118 (NIRSpec G395H) + [[JWST-PID-1541]] (NIRISS SOSS) reanalysis; H₂O detected at 3.09σ; demonstrates the need to reanalyze early commissioning data with updated pipeline calibrations.
