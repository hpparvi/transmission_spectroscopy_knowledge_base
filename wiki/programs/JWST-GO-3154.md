# JWST GO 3154

JWST Cycle 2 GO program 3154 (PI: E.-M. Ahrer); NIRSpec/G395H transmission spectroscopy of misaligned hot Jupiters orbiting host stars **above the Kraft break**, designed to test the Penzlin et al. 2024 prediction that high-eccentricity migration (misaligned) and disc-driven migration (aligned) leave distinct atmospheric C/O signatures. The program contributes part of a larger sample study; observation of the retrograde [[WASP-94Ab]] in this paper is a single ∼8 hr visit on 2024-06-07.

## Papers

- [[2025_Ahrer_WASP94Ab]] — single transit of WASP-94 Ab; substellar C/O = 0.49⁺⁰·⁰⁸⁻⁰·¹³ vs host C/O★ = 0.68 ± 0.10; stellar-like metallicity Z = 2× solar; favors pebble/planetesimal accretion + large-distance migration.
