# JWST GO 2722

JWST Cycle 1 GO program 2722 (PI: N. Madhusudhan); NIRISS/SOSS + NIRSpec/G395H transit observations of the temperate sub-Neptune [[K2-18b]]. Designed to test the **Hycean atmosphere hypothesis** — habitable sub-Neptunes with H₂-rich envelopes overlying liquid-water oceans. The original Madhusudhan et al. 2023 (not ingested) analysis reported CH₄, CO₂, and tentative dimethyl sulfide (DMS) — interpreted as a possible biosignature. The independent reanalyses [[2025_FernandezRodriguez_K2-18b]] and Schmidt et al. 2025 (not ingested) retract the DMS detection under comprehensive retrievals; CO₂ remains contested. The follow-up GO 2372 MIRI/LRS observations (Madhusudhan et al. 2025; not ingested) extend coverage to 12 μm.

## Papers

- [[2025_FernandezRodriguez_K2-18b]] — independent reanalysis with exoTEDRF + Eureka! reduction and TauREx3 retrievals across 12 spectrum variants; CH₄ at 4.16σ–4.62σ; CO₂ marginal at ~2σ; DMS retracted (Bayes factor < 1 in comprehensive retrievals); supersolar C/O.
