# JWST GO 2722

JWST Cycle 1 GO program 2722 (PI: N. Madhusudhan); NIRISS/SOSS + NIRSpec/G395H + MIRI/LRS transit observations of the temperate sub-Neptune [[K2-18b]]. Designed to test the **Hycean atmosphere hypothesis** — habitable sub-Neptunes with H₂-rich envelopes overlying liquid-water oceans. The original Madhusudhan et al. 2023 (not ingested) NIR analysis reported CH₄, CO₂, and tentative dimethyl sulfide (DMS) — interpreted as a possible biosignature. The MIRI follow-up ([[2025_Madhusudhan_K2-18b-MIRI]], 2024 April; ~5–12 μm) reasserts DMS+DMDS at 3σ in a 2-molecule canonical retrieval. Independent reanalyses ([[2025_FernandezRodriguez_K2-18b]], [[2025_Luque_K2-18b]], [[2025_PicaCiamarra_K2-18b]], [[2025_Schmidt_K2-18b]], plus Welbanks-Nixon 2025 not ingested) **retract the DMS detection** under comprehensive or broadened-candidate retrievals; CO₂ remains contested (shown to be reduction-specific in [[2025_Schmidt_K2-18b]]).

**Observations (all under GO-2722):**
- NIRISS SOSS — 2023 January 20–21, ~5.3 hr, 0.85–2.83 μm; spot-crossing event during ingress.
- NIRSpec G395H — 2023 June 1, ~4.9 hr, 2.8–5.2 μm.
- MIRI/LRS — 2024 April 25–26, 5.85 hr, ~5–12 μm.

## Papers

- [[2025_FernandezRodriguez_K2-18b]] — independent reanalysis with exoTEDRF + Eureka! reduction and TauREx3 retrievals across 12 spectrum variants; CH₄ at 4.16σ–4.62σ; CO₂ marginal at ~2σ; DMS retracted (Bayes factor < 1 in comprehensive retrievals); supersolar C/O.
- [[2025_Madhusudhan_K2-18b-MIRI]] — first MIRI transmission spectrum of K2-18 b; reports DMS+DMDS at 3.4σ over flat spectrum in canonical retrieval (CH₄+CO₂+X); retrieved log X(DMS), log X(DMDS) ≈ −3.5.
- [[2025_Luque_K2-18b]] — panchromatic joint NIRISS+NIRSpec+MIRI retrieval across three independent reductions (exoTEDRF, Eureka, SPARTA, JExoRES) and two retrieval codes (PLATON, SCARLET); DMS/DMDS never preferred at Δln Z ≥ 5 over baseline; ethane provides equally good fit; ~26 MIRI transits required for 3σ flat-line rejection.
- [[2025_PicaCiamarra_K2-18b]] — 650-molecule agnostic search; reproduces M25 DMS detection but identifies ~11 spectroscopically equivalent alternatives in MIRI (diethyl sulfide, methacrylonitrile, propyne, cyclohexane, butane, etc.).
- [[2025_Schmidt_K2-18b]] — comprehensive reanalysis of the GO-2722 NIRISS+NIRSpec data only (no MIRI reanalysis). Two NIRISS pipelines, four NIRSpec pipelines, **first analysis of NIRISS SOSS 2nd order**, two retrievals ([[POSEIDON]] + [[BeAR]]), >250 retrievals across 60 data combinations. CH₄ at 4.2σ; CO₂ max 2.3σ; DMS max 2.2σ; 95% upper limits log CO₂ < −1.70, log DMS < −3.70. Detects 5⁺³₋₂% cool starspot coverage. Argues oxygen-poor mini-Neptune (C/O ≈ 3.25) explains data without a liquid water ocean.
