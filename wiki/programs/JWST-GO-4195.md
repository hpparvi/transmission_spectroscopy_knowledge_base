# JWST-GO-4195

JWST GO program 4195 (PI: C. Fisher), targeting TOI-1685 b with NIRSpec G395H multi-visit transmission spectroscopy. Four new transits were obtained in 2024 (Feb 25, Feb 29, Mar 2, Sep 1), combined with one reanalyzed transit from the Luque et al. 2025 GO 3263 phase curve, for a total of five visits in [[2025_Fisher_TOI1685b]].

## Papers
- [[2025_Fisher_TOI1685b]] — five-visit NIRSpec G395H transmission of TOI-1685 b; flat spectrum; MMW ≳ 10 at ~5σ.
