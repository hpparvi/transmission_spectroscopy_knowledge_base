# JWST GO 2420

JWST Cycle 1 Guest Observer program 2420 (PI: A. D. Rathcke), targeting the TRAPPIST-1 system with [[JWST-NIRSpec]] PRISM to evaluate the presence of an atmosphere on [[TRAPPIST-1c]]. The final visit was strategically scheduled for 2024 July 9 to coincide with a quasi-simultaneous transit of [[TRAPPIST-1b]], enabling the [[back-to-back-transit-correction]] proof of concept.

## Papers
- [[2025_Rathcke_TRAPPIST1bc]] — back-to-back NIRSpec PRISM transits of TRAPPIST-1 b and c; demonstrates a 2.5× red-noise reduction in the c transmission spectrum after correction by b.
