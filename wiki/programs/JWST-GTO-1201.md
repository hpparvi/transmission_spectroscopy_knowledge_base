# JWST GTO 1201

JWST Cycle 1 Guaranteed Time Observations program 1201 (PI: D. Lafrenière), part of the **NEAT** survey (Near-infrared Exploration of the Atmospheric diversity of Transiting exoplanets). Uses both [[JWST-NIRISS]] SOSS (0.85–2.8 μm) and [[JWST-NIRSpec]] PRISM (0.6–5.2 μm) to observe transiting rocky, sub-Neptune, and gas-giant planets across a range of host spectral types. Multi-target survey; confirmed targets include [[GJ357b]], TRAPPIST-1 d, L 98-59 b/d (Damiano et al. 2024 for L 98-59 b; not yet ingested), the warm super-puff [[WASP-107b]] (M. M. Murphy et al. 2025; V. Krishnamurthy et al. 2025), and the hot Jupiter [[WASP-52b]] (M. Fournier-Tondreau et al. 2025). NIRISS reductions use the [[exoTEDRF]] pipeline; NIRSpec reductions use [[Eureka]] and [[stctm]].

## Papers
- [[2025_PiauletGhorayeb_TRAPPIST1d]] — two NIRSpec PRISM transits of TRAPPIST-1 d (Nov 5+9 2022); flat spectrum; H₂ excluded >3σ; cloud-free terrestrial analogs excluded >95%; N₂-rich consistent.
- [[2025_Taylor_GJ357b]] — single NIRISS/SOSS transit of GJ 357 b on 2023-11-22; featureless spectrum.
- [[2025_Roy_LP791-18c]] — single NIRSpec PRISM transit of the temperate sub-Neptune LP 791-18 c; strong haze + CH₄ detection; first wiki characterization of an LP 791-18 system planet.
- [[2025_Howard_TRAPPIST1_flares]] — partial source data: NIRSpec PRISM observation of TRAPPIST-1 f reanalyzed for flare characterization in the methodology paper.
- [[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]] — uses GTO 1201 NIRISS/SOSS data on [[WASP-107b]] and [[WASP-52b]] in the nine-planet limb-asymmetry reanalysis.
