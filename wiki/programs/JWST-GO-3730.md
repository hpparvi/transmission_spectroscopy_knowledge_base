# JWST-GO-3730

JWST Cycle 2 GO program 3730 (PI: H. Diamond-Lowe; Co-PI: J. M. Mendonça), the **Hot Rocks Survey** — a MIRI F1500W photometric secondary-eclipse survey of nine rocky planets around M dwarfs designed to characterize atmospheric retention versus the [[cosmic-shoreline]]. Targets include LHS 1478 b, TOI-1468 b, LHS 1140 c, GJ 3473 b, LTT 3780 b, plus several others. The series of target-by-target analyses is published as Hot Rocks Survey papers I–V; only paper V ([[2026_Holmberg_GJ3473b]]) is ingested in the wiki to date.

## Papers

- [[2026_Holmberg_GJ3473b]] — Hot Rocks Survey **V**: four MIRI F1500W eclipses of GJ 3473 b; joint depth 186 ± 45 ppm; R = 0.82 ± 0.12; both bare-rock and atmosphere consistent.
- [[2025_Connors_MIRI-15um]] — uniform FN-PCA reanalysis of three Hot Rocks targets (LHS 1478 b, TOI-1468 b, LHS 1140 c) plus TRAPPIST-1 b/c; methodology paper for the upcoming [[JWST-DD-9235]] Rocky Worlds DDT.
