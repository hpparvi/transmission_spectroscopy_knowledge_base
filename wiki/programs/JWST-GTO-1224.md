# JWST GTO 1224

JWST Cycle 1 Guaranteed Time Observation program 1224 (PI: S. Birkmann), observing the L 98-59 system with NIRSpec/G395H. The single transit of [[L98-59d]] obtained under this program has been independently reanalyzed in companion papers [[2024_Banerjee_L98-59d]] and [[2024_Gressier_L98-59d]], both finding evidence for an out-of-equilibrium secondary atmosphere with [[H2S]] and [[SO2]].

## Papers
- [[2024_Gressier_L98-59d]] — companion "Paper I" on the L 98-59 d transit.
- [[2024_Banerjee_L98-59d]] — companion "Paper II" on the same transit with different retrieval code.
