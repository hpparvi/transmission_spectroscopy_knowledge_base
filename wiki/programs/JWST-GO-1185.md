# JWST GO 1185

JWST Cycle 1 GO program 1185 (PI: E. Schlawin), companion to [[JWST-GO-1177]] in the **MANATEE survey**. Two [[JWST-MIRI]] LRS eclipse observations of the warm Neptune [[GJ436b]] covering 5–12 μm.

## Papers

- [[2025_Mukherjee_GJ436b]] — combined GO-1177 NIRCam (2.4–5.0 μm) + GO-1185 MIRI LRS (5–12 μm) eclipses provide the first panchromatic 2.4–12 μm thermal emission spectrum of GJ 436 b.
