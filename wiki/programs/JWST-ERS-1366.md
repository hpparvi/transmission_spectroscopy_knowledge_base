# JWST-ERS-1366

JWST Early Release Science program 1366 (PI: N. Batalha), targeting WASP-39 b as the benchmark hot-Jupiter characterization dataset for the JWST era. Multi-instrument transmission spectroscopy including NIRSpec PRISM, NIRSpec G395H, NIRSpec G235H, NIRISS/SOSS, and NIRCam; observed 2022 July 10 (PRISM). The ERS data produced the first JWST detection of CO₂ in an exoplanet atmosphere and the first identification of SO₂ via photochemistry (original ERS team papers; not yet ingested). The PRISM dataset has since been re-analyzed by independent groups.

## Papers

- [[2026_RoyPerez_WASP39b]] — data-reduction systematics study using the ERS PRISM transit; four pipelines, six reductions; retrieved abundances vary by up to 2 dex.
