# JWST GO 1177

JWST Cycle 1 GO program 1177 (PI: E. Schlawin), part of the **MANATEE survey** (Methodology to Analyze NIRCam And Time-series spectroscopy of Exoplanets). Multi-eclipse program of the warm Neptune [[GJ436b]] using [[JWST-NIRCam]] F322W2 and F444W grism modes. Companion to [[JWST-GO-1185]] which adds [[JWST-MIRI]] LRS coverage.

## Papers

- [[2025_Mukherjee_GJ436b]] — combined GO-1177 (3× NIRCam F322W2 + 3× NIRCam F444W) + GO-1185 (2× MIRI LRS) panchromatic 2.4–12 μm thermal emission spectrum of GJ 436 b; overturns Spitzer-era extreme-metallicity / hot-interior inference; revised [M/H] ≥ 80–500× solar, T_int ~ 60 K.
