# JWST GO 5924

JWST Cycle 3 GO program 5924; NIRISS/SOSS transmission spectroscopy of warm-to-hot gas giants. Includes [[WASP-80b]] (C. Gascon, in prep) and [[WASP-94Ab]] (S. Mukherjee et al. 2025, submitted) as targets relevant to the [[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]] limb-asymmetry sample.

## Papers
- [[2025_Fu_limb-asymmetry]] — uses archival GO 5924 NIRISS/SOSS data on WASP-80 b and WASP-94 Ab as part of the nine-planet uniform reanalysis.
